About ReactLab
Built for students who want to learn chemistry beyond the textbook.

ReactLab is an interactive educational platform designed to help students understand the mechanistic principles behind classic named reactions in organic chemistry.

Each reaction page provides a detailed step-by-step mechanism, a real-time 3D molecular viewer powered by Three.js, and an interactive conditions panel where you can adjust temperature, pressure, and concentration to see kinetic effects in practice.

Whether you're preparing for JEE, NEET, university finals, or just passionate about chemistry — ReactLab makes complex reactions intuitive and visual.
